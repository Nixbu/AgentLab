The History of Tomatoes

The tomato is the edible berry of the plant Solanum lycopersicum, commonly known as the tomato plant. It originated in western South America and Central America using the Nahuatl word tomatl.

Tomatoes are distinct from other vegetables in that they feature a high water content and are rich in Vitamin C.
